About Edural Classes

Quality Education for Every Student

Edural Classes is an innovative educational initiative focused on transforming education in Tier 3 & 4 cities and rural India. By combining expert educators, modern digital tools, and structured academic systems, Edural Classes aims to deliver urban-quality education regardless of geographical limitations.

Why This Student Performance Dashboard?

Performance Transparency

This dashboard enables students to clearly understand their academic progress through visual indicators like charts, rankings, and summaries.

Teacher-Guided Monitoring

Teachers can record and manage marks and attendance, ensuring structured academic tracking for every student.

Data-Driven Learning

By visualizing performance data, students can identify strengths, weaknesses, and improvement areas early.

Support for Rural Education

This system bridges the academic monitoring gap often faced in rural and semi-urban education environments.

Our Mission

To bridge the educational divide by providing access to expert teachers, digital classrooms, and structured performance monitoring systems that help students grow with confidence.

Our Vision

To empower students across India with technology-driven education tools that promote accountability, self-analysis, and academic excellence.
